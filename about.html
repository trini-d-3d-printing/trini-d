About Trini-D

Trini-D is built for customers who already have 3D model files and need them printed with care, clarity, and reliable communication.

Our Focus

Three dimensions, endless possibilities.

We provide 3D printing services for ready-made models such as prototypes, replacement parts, display items, gifts, educational models, and business needs.

Our goal is simple: make it easy to send a model file, understand the cost, and receive a printed item with good quality and finishing.

Accepted files: .stl and .3mf
WhatsApp-first communication
Clear quotation before printing
Quality-focused printing workflow

Important Service Note

This website is written for a printing-focused business. It does not promote 3D designing as a main service. Customers should send ready-made 3D model files for quotation.
